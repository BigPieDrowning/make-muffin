How to make a Muffin Tutorial

This is a step-by-step tutorial on how to make delicious muffins!

List of ingredients that you will need to make the muffins:

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Oil

Follow the steps below:

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.

  2. In another bowl, mix eggs, milk, and oil.

  3. Stir both mixtures together.

  4. Fill muffin tray 3/4 full.

  5. Bake for 20 minutes.

After doing all that, you can now eat the muffins you baked with love.
